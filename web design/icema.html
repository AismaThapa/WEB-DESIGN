list of tourism place in Nepal

sworgadwari temple

Sworgadwari is a hilltop temple and pilgrimage site in Pyuthan District, Nepal, commemorating the special role of cows in Hinduism.
It is said to have been founded by Guru Maharaj Narayan Khatri.

pashupatinath temple

Pashupatinath Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, and is located in Kathmandu, Nepal. This temple was classified as a World Heritage Site in 1979
